HPACKIndexing

public enum HPACKIndexing : CustomStringConvertible

Defines the types of indexing and rewriting operations a decoder may take with regard to this header.

  • indexable

    Header may be written into the dynamic index table or may be rewritten by proxy servers.

    Declaration

    Swift

    case indexable
  • nonIndexable

    Header is not written to the dynamic index table, but proxies may rewrite it en-route, as long as they preserve its non-indexable attribute.

    Declaration

    Swift

    case nonIndexable
  • neverIndexed

    Header may not be written to the dynamic index table, and proxies must pass it on as-is without rewriting.
