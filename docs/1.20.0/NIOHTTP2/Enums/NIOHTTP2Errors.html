  • StreamError

    A StreamError was hit during outbound frame processing.

    Stream errors are wrappers around another error of some other kind that occurred on a specific stream. As they are a wrapper error, they carry a “real” error in the baseError. Additionally, they cannot meaningfully be Equatable, so they aren’t. There’s also no additional location information: that’s provided by the base error.
